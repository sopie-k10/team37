About Us

01Who We Are

Chilli Pepper Robotics develops skilled, forward-thinking students through a strong emphasis on collaboration, leadership, and the real-world application of STEM principles — preparing them to excel in both competitive robotics and future engineering careers.

🏆

Award-Winning

Our members have earned well over a hundred awards across regional, provincial, and international VEX competition.

🤖

Eight Teams

Eight competitive teams that consistently demonstrate advanced design, strategy, and high-level performance.

📍

Across Two Countries

Based in Ottawa, Toronto, and Vancouver in Canada, and in Cary, Tulsa, and Highland Park in the USA.
